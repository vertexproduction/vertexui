The Game

What is Farm House RNG?

Farm House RNG is a luck-based Roblox experience set on a sprawling countryside estate. Every roll of the RNG machine can yield anything from a rusty pitchfork to an otherworldly Divine Sunflower — it's all down to chance, patience, and a little bit of farm magic.

Collect hundreds of unique farm items, unlock hidden auras, climb the leaderboard, and flex your rarest pulls. No two runs are ever the same.

Got Questions?

FAQ

How do I get better drop rates?

You can brew Luck Potions in the Potion Shed, buy them from other players at the Farmer's Market, or earn them from daily login rewards. Stacking potions multiplies their effect up to 5×.

Are there any secret items?

Yes! There are 5 hidden "????" items that only appear under specific conditions — certain times of day, weather events, or after reaching milestones. The community wiki has partial clues.

When does the season reset?

Seasons last approximately 60 days. The leaderboard resets at the start of each new season but your inventory and unlocked items carry over permanently.

Can I trade or gift items to other players?

No — gifting and trading are fully disabled in Farm House RNG. All items must be earned through your own rolls. This keeps the game fair and ensures every inventory is 100% self-earned.

Is the game mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. Farm House RNG is fully playable on Roblox mobile. The roll button is large and all UI elements are touch-optimised.

How do I report a bug?

Join the official Discord server (link in-game) and post in #bug-reports. Include your username, the issue, and screenshots if possible. We aim to patch within 48 hours.
